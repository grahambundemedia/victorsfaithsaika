Global Mission

ENENDENI OPERATION

The ENENDENI OPERATION is an operation set up to win for the body of christ through our door to door evangelism.

The operation was set to start in the year 2005 when the church began. The enendeni operation target is to win more than 3,000 souls to Christ throught preaching and evangelism and baptism. To boost this operation we have built more churches in Kenya and worldwide and we have preachers and evangelists worldwide.

We also have collaborated with world mission partners such as PAIS to continue spreading the word of life to the nations.
